Critical & Chronic Illness

0% Floor Protection
In 2008 the S&P lost 38.49%. Your IRA lost 38.49%. The IUL credited 0% — no loss. You can't lose what you've already earned. The floor resets every year.

Penalty-Free Access Before 59½
IRA withdrawals before 59½ trigger a 10% penalty plus income tax. Policy loans from life insurance are available at any age with no penalties and no tax consequences.

In most states, life insurance cash values are protected from creditors, lawsuits, and bankruptcy proceedings. IRAs have limited protection that varies by state.

📋
No Required Minimum Distributions
Starting at 73, the IRS forces you to withdraw from your IRA whether you want to or not — creating taxable events. Life insurance has no RMDs. You choose when and how much to take.
