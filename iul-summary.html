The Spread — What the Carrier Can't Show

AG49-A regulations restrict carrier illustrations to a 0.5% spread assumption. The Liberty Pointe Method™ uses the participating loan feature to capture the spread between your 5% fixed loan cost and the index crediting rate — compounding annually over the life of the policy. This additional growth is not reflected in the carrier illustration.
