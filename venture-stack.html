Private — Concept Overview

A Three-Company Stack Built to Compound

Three complementary entities. Each one strengthens the others. Together, they form a self-reinforcing loop from first client contact to long-term financial relationship.

The Structure

How the Three Entities Work Together

Each company has a distinct role. No overlap. No competition. Every client interaction creates value that flows through all three.

Layer 1 — The Engine

Digital Marketing & Management Co.

Infrastructure · Growth · Automation

Builds and manages the complete digital presence for the law firm — website, SEO, Google Business, social media, AI tools, and automated lead funnels. Charges a one-time setup fee and an ongoing monthly retainer. Also eligible for a revenue-share arrangement in lieu of, or in addition to, a retainer.

Website Build SEO & Google Social Media AI Chatbot Lead Funnels CRM & Automation Monthly Retainer
Drives Traffic & Delivers Clients
Layer 2 — The Front Door

Estate Planning Law Firm

Legal · Trust · Credibility

A licensed, attorney-led estate planning firm. Provides revocable trusts, wills, powers of attorney, healthcare directives, and business entity documents. High-trust entry point — clients come to an attorney, the most credible professional in the room. Once the estate plan is complete, the full financial picture comes into view.

Revocable Trusts Wills & POA Healthcare Directives Minor Protection Business Services 95% Close Rate
Warm Referral — Client Financial Review
Layer 3 — The Back End

Liberty Pointe Financial

Insurance · Annuities · Wealth Strategy

Independent financial firm specializing in IUL (Indexed Universal Life), annuities, and retirement income strategy. Receives warm referrals from the law firm — clients who have already opened up about their assets, goals, and family situation. No cold leads. Highest-quality prospects in the funnel. Contracted with 12+ carriers for unbiased recommendations.

IUL / Life Insurance Fixed Annuities Income Riders Tax Mitigation Retirement Income 12+ Carriers
