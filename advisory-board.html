The Methodology Behind It

Built on the principles of
All-Source Intelligence

In the intelligence community, analysts long ago discovered that no single source of information tells the whole story. HUMINT (human intelligence) reveals what people know and intend. SIGINT captures communications. GEOINT shows movement and position. OSINT surfaces what's publicly visible. Each discipline sees something the others miss entirely.

All-Source Fusion Intelligence was developed to solve this problem — by deliberately gathering input from every available source and synthesizing it into a single, coherent, actionable picture. The result is dramatically more accurate than any one source alone. It's the difference between seeing a fragment of the puzzle and seeing the whole board.

The Liberty Pointe Advisory Board applies this same discipline to your financial life. Estate planning. Insurance. Retirement income. Legal protection. Tax strategy. Financial planning and investments. Real estate and REITs. Each specialist sees your situation through a different lens — and catches what the others would miss. We gather every perspective, synthesize them together, and deliver you one complete picture. Not disconnected opinions. A fused strategy.

The Process

How the Advisory Board Works

A structured, rigorous process — from your first conversation to a fully unified strategy in your hands.

1

The Intake: Your Complete Financial Picture

You meet privately with Chris — your Advisory Board coordinator. Together you map everything: goals, assets, liabilities, income, family situation, risk tolerance, timelines. No forms dropped in a portal. A real conversation. This is your intelligence briefing — and it drives everything that follows.

2

The Board Reviews Your Case

Chris packages your complete financial profile and shares it with the relevant specialists on the Advisory Board. Each expert analyzes it through their lens — estate planning, retirement income, insurance protection, legal structure, tax efficiency. They provide their independent perspective and recommendations.

3

The Fusion: One Unified Proposal

Chris synthesizes every expert's input into a single, coherent strategy document — not five separate reports that contradict each other. You see where the experts agree, where they differ, and why. You understand the trade-offs. You leave with clarity, not more confusion.

4

Your Review & Expert Introductions

Chris presents the unified proposal to you in a dedicated review meeting. You ask questions. You decide which recommendations to act on. For anything you want to move forward with, Chris makes the personal introduction to the relevant expert — and stays involved throughout to ensure the strategy stays cohesive.
